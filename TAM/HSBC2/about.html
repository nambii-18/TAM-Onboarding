About The Tool

The Ticket Management & Assignment Tool streamlines the process of assigning customer support qmtester to engineers.
The Queue Managerâ€™s Dashboard gives them the provision to append engineers to their shift, and assign customer support qmtester to the engineers. The Engineerâ€™s Dashboard gives the engineers the provision to view, accept & reject cases assigned to them. The tool also provides personalized push notifications for the engineer as and when a case is assigned to them.
The engineer can choose to reject a case with a valid reason. The QM then sees an option to reassign this rejected case.
The tool maintains a record of all historical cases and has reporting/visualization capabilities built into it.
